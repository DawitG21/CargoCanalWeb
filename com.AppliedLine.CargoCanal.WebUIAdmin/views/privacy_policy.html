Customer Data

CargoCanal is a cloud service that stores operational and financial information about a company or individual (“Organization”) as provided or entered by that company or individual. The data entered, or imported by you remains your property and CargoCanal will not use nor make available for use any of this information without your permission.

The data entered or imported by you is stored securely in a database and is only accessible to any person you have authorized to use the Services. It is your responsibility to keep your password safe. CargoCanal, CargoCanal's staff and CargoCanal's partners do not have access to your password and are therefore unable to access your Organization's account or data without receiving a specific invitation to do so from you.

CargoCanal will not review, share, distribute, or reference any of the data entered and stored in the Services except if required by law. In accordance with the CargoCanal terms of service, CargoCanal staff may access customer data only for the purposes of providing the Services, and preventing or addressing service or technical problems, at the customer's request in connection to customer support matters, or as may be required by law.

CargoCanal staff and key commercial partners can access non-identifying and aggregated usage information and transaction volumes in order to better understand how customers are using the Services, in order to improve the system design and where appropriate have the system prompt users with suggestions on ways to improve their own use of the system or service. All aggregated usage information is stored in a secure CargoCanal data warehouse facility.

Provided you have met your obligations under CargoCanal's Terms of Service, on request, CargoCanal will provide you with a full export of the data in a common file format determined by CargoCanal. Access to the data may be permanently deleted by CargoCanal 30 days after you cease payment for the Services or at your request.

CargoCanal stores your data in the location CargoCanal considers will give you the most efficient access from wherever in the world you may be. When you access or input data from somewhere other than the country where the data is stored or to be stored, you consent to that data being transferred from one country to the other (including via any intermediate country) as a function of transmission across the internet.

CargoCanal has SSL certificates issued by leading certificate authorities, so all data transferred between users and the Service is encrypted. Your browser must support the encryption security used in connection with the Services.
